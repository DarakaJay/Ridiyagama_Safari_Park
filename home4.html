A little bit about Ridiyagama Safari Park Sri Lanka

Welcome to Ridiyagama Safari Park, one of Sri Lanka's largest wildlife parks, designed to give you an unforgettable adventure. Located in the Southern province of Sri Lanka, this park offers a perfect blend of nature, wildlife, and adventure, providing visitors with an immersive safari experience.

The park is thoughtfully divided into different sections, each representing various African and Sri Lankan wildlife environments. It features expansive open-range safari zones, where visitors can witness animals roam freely in environments resembling their natural habitats. The park is home to majestic animals such as lions, giraffes, zebras, and elephants.

Embark on a guided safari tour where our experienced rangers will take you through the different zones, sharing insights about the animals, their behaviors, and the conservation efforts in place. Our mission is to provide both educational and thrilling experiences to all visitors, from families to wildlife enthusiasts.

Planning your Ridiyagama Safari Adventure

Part of the excitement of visiting Ridiyagama Safari Park is planning your adventure. With a variety of experiences to choose from, you can tailor your visit to match your interests. Whether you're interested in a thrilling safari ride, a peaceful bird-watching tour, or even a visit to the park's dedicated educational center, there's something for everyone.

To make your trip seamless, we offer multiple booking options. You can book your tickets online or at the park entrance, and we also recommend booking your safari tours in advance to ensure availability. Our tours cater to all levels of adventure seekers, from short guided drives to full-day tours. Our team is always ready to assist in planning the perfect day out.

And if you're still unsure about what to do, our expert guides are available to help you customize your experience based on your preferences. From educational talks to up-close animal encounters, we promise an unforgettable experience!
